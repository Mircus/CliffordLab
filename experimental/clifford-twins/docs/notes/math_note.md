# Math note

The main construction is:

\[
H=\mathrm{Cl}^+(3,0)\cong\mathbb H,
\qquad
\mathrm{Cl}(3,0)=H\oplus HI.
\]

The same 8-dimensional vector space carries two products.

## Passive product

\[
Ia=aI.
\]

\[
(a+bI)(c+dI)=(ac-bd)+(ad+bc)I.
\]

This is the usual associative Clifford product in the split \(H\oplus HI\).

## Active product

\[
\ell a=\bar a\ell.
\]

\[
(a+b\ell)\star(c+d\ell)
=
(ac-\bar d\,b)+(da+b\bar c)\ell.
\]

This is the octonion product.

## Folded triality

The octonionic 8-space can be used as any of the three triality faces:

\[
V\cong\mathbb O,
\qquad
S_+\cong\mathbb O,
\qquad
S_-\cong\mathbb O.
\]

In the large Clifford picture, triality is unfolded inside the representation architecture of \(\mathrm{Cl}(8,0)\). In the octonionic picture, triality is folded into one compact nonassociative 8D body.
